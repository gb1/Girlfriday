ABOUT GIRL FRIDAY

Girl Friday is here to help with almost any home, office or pet based task and take the pressure off your busy life.

Available on an ad-hoc or more permanent basis I aim to offer complete flexibility to meet your needs. This could be one off support for a project or weekly/monthly support for ongoing tasks. Let me help with anything from office/home admin, running errands and dog walking to watering plants and home checks whilst you are on holiday. Having spent 12 years working in the fast-paced Fashion Industry, I set up Girl Friday to allow a little more flexibility around my family life and focus on the areas of my previous career and personal life I love the most.

I am a personable, versatile and highly motivated professional and I thrive on being organised, working under pressure and variety. I have a vast range of knowledge from my previous career and am now a professional multi-tasker!

My customers include private individuals requiring lifestyle support and business owners requiring PA and operational support.

I will meet with you to discuss your specific needs and requirements. I understand the demand for support does fluctuate and therefore I offer a bespoke service that requires a minimum time commitment of only an hour. My services can be used for as little or as long as required and on an ad-hoc basis
